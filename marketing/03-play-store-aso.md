## Reviews strategy (first 30 days)

**The first 20 reviews matter more than the next 200.**

Day 1: Text 30 friends/family personally. Ask them to download AND leave a written review (not just a star rating). Even 5-10 honest reviews in week 1 changes how Google's algorithm classifies your app.

Don't ask for 5-stars. Ask for honest reviews. Honest reviews from real users will average 4.5+ if the game is good, and Google trusts the variance more than uniform 5-stars.

**Reply to every review** — positive AND negative — in the first 30 days. Reply rate is a public ranking signal.
