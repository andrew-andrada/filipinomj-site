# Creator + Press Outreach Templates

Three templates: warm creator pitch, cold creator pitch, and Filipino-American media outlet pitch.

**Rule of thumb:** Personalize the first sentence of every email. Generic sends get ignored. Even one sentence proving you've watched their content lifts response rate dramatically.

---

# 1. WARM creator pitch (Filipino TikTokers, YouTubers, Instagram personalities)

**Use when:** You've watched their content, can reference something specific, and they have a Filipino audience.

- A Pinoy mahjong app I think your community would love
- I built a Filipino mahjong game — would mean a lot if you tried it
- For your Pinoy followers — first authentic Filipino mahjong app

Hi [Name],

I'm Andrew Andrada — a Pinoy developer based in [your city]. I just launched **Filipino Mahjong: Joker Rules**, a free Android app that plays the authentic 17-tile Filipino mahjong our titos and titas play at family gatherings.

- I've been watching your videos on [specific topic] for a while — your stuff on [specific reference] hit me especially hard.

No payment expected, no pressure to post — just thought you'd enjoy playing it. If you do and feel like sharing it with your followers, I'd be honored.

# 5. Target list — who to email first

**Tier 1: Filipino-content TikTokers / IG creators (warm pitch)**

Spend 2-3 evenings finding Filipino creators with 10K-200K followers who've posted about:
- Family gatherings
- Filipino food / cooking
- Filipino-American identity
- Tagalog-language content
- Pinoy gaming or board games

Smaller creators (10K-100K) reply more often than mega-influencers and have more engaged audiences.

Suggested seeds — search these on TikTok/IG and follow the algorithm trails:
- #FilipinoTok
- #PinoyTok
- #FilipinoAmerican
- #FilipinoCommunity
- #BalikbayanBox
- #FilipinoFood
- #PinoyGamers

**Tier 2: Filipino-American newspapers**
- Asian Journal (asianjournal.com)
- Bayanihan News
- The FilAm
- Philippine News
- Filipino Reporter (filipinoreporter.us)

Their tech/lifestyle editors love human-interest tech stories about Filipino-American makers.

**Tier 3: Pinoy podcasters**
Search Spotify/Apple Podcasts for "Filipino" + "American" + "diaspora." Reach out to anyone with 50+ episodes (proves they'll keep their show alive long enough to publish your interview).

**Tier 4: Filipino Reddit moderators (DMs, not posts)**

Mods of r/Filipino, r/Philippines, r/FilipinoAmerican sometimes pin or AMA-feature interesting community projects. Send a courteous DM offering an AMA or pinned launch post — don't expect a yes, but it's worth one shot.

# 6. Tracking

Make a simple spreadsheet (one row per outreach):
- Name | Platform | Followers | Date emailed | Personalization line | Reply (Y/N) | Result | Notes

Even 30 emails out, 2-3 replies, 1 share — that's a successful campaign for a free indie game. Keep expectations calibrated.
