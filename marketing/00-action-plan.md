# Launch Action Plan — Step-by-step

Your game is **live on Google Play**. You have all the marketing assets written but haven't used them yet. This is the playbook.

Read this top to bottom. Then start at **Day 0** and don't skip steps. Most steps are short — the ones that matter aren't the long ones.

---

## The one rule that matters most

**Reply rate beats reach.** Every comment. Every DM. Every Reddit reply. Every Play Store review. For the first 30 days, spend 30 minutes a day replying before you do anything else. Google's Play Store algorithm and Reddit's algorithm both weight engagement velocity. A post with 12 thoughtful replies will outperform a post with 200 likes and no replies.

If you do nothing else from this plan but reply to every interaction for 30 days, you'll be ahead of most indie launches.

---

## Day 0 — Setup (today, ~90 min total)

You're not posting anything publicly yet. You're getting the foundation right so when you do start posting, the funnel works.

### 1. Tracking spreadsheet (10 min)
Open a Google Sheet. Three tabs:

**Tab 1: Outreach** — columns: `Name | Platform | Followers | Date emailed | Personalization line | Reply? (Y/N) | Result | Notes`

**Tab 2: Posts** — columns: `Date | Channel | Post (which one from content pack) | Link | Impressions | Engagements | Comments | DMs received | Notes`

**Tab 3: Reviews** — columns: `Date | Star rating | Reviewer name | Review text | Did I reply? | My reply`

This is the only spreadsheet you need. Don't overcomplicate.

### 2. Play Store listing optimization (30 min)
Open Play Console → your app → Main store listing. Update with the ASO copy from `03-play-store-aso.md`:

- [ ] **App name** — confirm "Filipino Mahjong: Joker Rules" (29 chars)
- [ ] **Short description** — paste the 74-char one: "Authentic Pinoy mahjong with Jokers, Bisaklat, 17 tiles. Free, no sign-up."
- [ ] **Long description** — paste the full 3,650-char block. It includes the v1.5 named bots, the 7-tier rank ladder, achievements, daily challenges. **This is the biggest single ASO lever you have right now.**
- [ ] **Screenshots order** — confirm your strongest 3 screenshots are first (hero gameplay, Bisaklat win, Joker reveal). The first 3 decide whether people install.

Save changes. Play Store may take a few hours to a day to push the new listing live.

### 3. Instagram bio refresh (15 min)
Open `@filipinomahjonggame`. Update bio to:

```
🀄 Authentic Filipino Mahjong: Joker Rules
17 tiles · Jokers · Bisaklat · Sagása
Play with Lola Winnie, Tita Nene & Kuya Edgar
📱 Free on Google Play
🍎 iPhone coming soon
👇
filipinomj.com
```

Set the link-in-bio to `filipinomj.com`. (If you want analytics, route through a Linktree/Beacons later. Don't bother for week 1.)

Profile photo: your `Images/App Icon_3.png` (or v6 logo).

### 4. Personal outreach list (30 min)
Open your phone contacts. Make a list of every Pinoy person you know — family, friends, coworkers, classmates. Aim for **20-40 names**.

These are the people who will leave your first reviews. **The first 10-20 reviews matter more than the next 200** because they teach Google's algorithm what your app is and who plays it.

Don't text them yet. Just list them. You'll text them in Day 1.

## Day 1 — Personal network blast (the most important day, ~2 hours)

Today is reviews day. No public posts yet.

### 5. Text everyone on your personal list (90 min)
Send each person individually. Don't group-text — group-texts feel like spam and get ignored.

Use this template, lightly personalized per person:

> Hey [name]! Quick favor — I built a Filipino mahjong app and just launched it on Google Play. It's the version we actually play at home (Jokers, Bisaklat, the whole thing). Would mean a lot if you'd try it and leave an honest review on the Play Store. No pressure if mahjong isn't your thing. Link: [PLAY_STORE_LINK]. Salamat!

Variations:
- For family elders: more "po," more deferential, mention Lola/Tita/Tito connection
- For friends: more casual, joke about how you've been talking about this for months
- For coworkers: keep it brief and professional, just the one ask

Track responses in your Outreach tab. Don't ghost anyone — reply to thank yous, answer questions, follow up gently after a few days if they said they'd try it.

### 6. Reply to every Play Store review the same day they appear (rolling)
Open Play Console → Reviews. Reply to every review within 24 hours for the first 30 days. This is non-negotiable.

Reply style:
- Thank them by name
- Respond to something specific they said
- If they raised an issue: acknowledge it, say what you're doing about it
- If they raved: be warm, not gushing. Maybe mention iPhone is coming.

Even a one-sentence "Salamat, [name]! So glad Bisaklat hit for you" matters. Reply rate is a public signal Google's algorithm watches.

## Day 2-3 — First public posts (2 hours over two days)

### 7. Instagram launch post (45 min, Day 2 evening)
From `02-launch-content-pack.md`, take **Post 1** (the launch announcement). Pair it with your strongest in-game screenshot (probably `FMJ5.jpeg` showing a big win, or `FMJ1.jpeg` showing the table). Post it.

After posting:
- Share it to your Story with a "Mga kababayan, it's here!" overlay
- DM 5 close friends with the link — ask them to share or comment
- Reply to every single comment for the next 24 hours

### 8. Reddit posts (45 min, spread across Day 2-3)
From `02-launch-content-pack.md`, post to **r/Filipino** and **r/Philippines** on Day 2. Wait until Day 3 to post to r/AndroidGaming and r/Mahjong (don't burn all four at once — Reddit recommendation algos punish "spam launches").

**Critical Reddit rule:** Before posting your launch thread, have **at least one prior comment** on each subreddit from your account. If you're posting cold from an account with zero history on r/Filipino, the post will be removed by mods. Spend 10 minutes scrolling each sub and leaving 1-2 sincere comments on other people's posts first.

Posting time matters: Mondays through Thursdays, 7-10pm Pacific gets the most Filipino-American eyeballs. Avoid weekends and Friday nights — much less reach.

### 9. Facebook Filipino groups (1 hour, Day 3)
From `02-launch-content-pack.md`, take the Facebook group post. Post it in **2-3 groups**, not 10. Cold-posting in 10 groups simultaneously gets you flagged as spam in all of them.

Before posting in a group:
- Join the group at least a day earlier (better: a week earlier)
- Read pinned rules — some groups ban self-promotion completely
- If the rules allow it, post at a low-activity time (Tuesday morning is good)
- Don't tag people in your post — that reads as spammy

Best groups to start with (search Facebook for these):
- "Mga Pinoy sa [your city]"
- "Filipinos in [your country/state]"
- "Mahjong Philippines" / "Filipino Mahjong Players"
- "Pinoy Gaming Community"

## Day 4-7 — Sustain (30 min/day)

You're not posting new content every day. You're replying.

### 10. Daily reply ritual (30 min/day)
Every evening, do this in order:
1. Play Store reviews → reply to every new one
2. Instagram comments and DMs
3. Reddit thread replies (your launch threads should still be getting comments for 3-5 days)
4. Facebook group comments
5. Any DMs across all platforms

If you have time after replies, post one Instagram Story showing something small — a daily challenge you completed, a screenshot of someone's review, a behind-the-scenes shot of your dev setup.

### 11. Drop **Post 2 (Bisaklat hype)** mid-week (Day 4 or 5)
From `02-launch-content-pack.md`. Pair with a screenshot of a Bisaklat win. Same routine: reply to every comment.

## Week 2 — First Reel, more sustained posting (~3 hours total)

### 12. Record your first TikTok / Instagram Reel (Day 8-10)
Start with **Script 1 — "POV: family mahjong night"** from `02-launch-content-pack.md`. It's the easiest to shoot (just phone-screen footage), works without you on camera, and the hook is universal.

Tools you need:
- Phone screen recorder (built into iOS/Android)
- CapCut (free, on phone) for editing
- A short voiceover (record on Voice Memos)

15-20 second video. Don't overthink it. Post to TikTok and Instagram Reels simultaneously.

### 13. Post 4 (the "3 calls" reel) — Day 12
Slightly more work — requires three gameplay clips. Same routine.

### 14. Personal follow-up wave (Day 10)
Check your Day 1 personal outreach list. Anyone who said "I'll try it later" but hasn't reviewed yet — send one gentle follow-up:

> Hey [name], not pushing you on this, but if you got a chance to try the mahjong app I'd really value your honest take. Even a one-line review on the Play Store helps a ton. Either way, salamat!

One follow-up per person. Not two.

## Week 3 — Creator outreach (3-4 hours over the week)

This is where you start scaling beyond your personal network.

### 15. Build a creator target list (90 min, Day 15-16)
From `04-creator-outreach.md`, find **15-20 Filipino creators** to email. Open Instagram and TikTok. Search:
- #FilipinoTok
- #PinoyTok
- #FilipinoAmerican
- #FilipinoCommunity

Look for creators with **10K-150K followers** specifically. Mega-influencers don't reply. Smaller creators (under 10K) don't have enough reach to be worth the email cost. The 10K-150K range is the sweet spot.

For each one, in your Outreach tab note:
- Their handle
- One specific video of theirs you actually watched
- One sentence about why their content resonates with you (this becomes your personalization line)

### 16. Send 5 warm pitches per evening (Day 17-19)
Use the **warm creator pitch** template from `04-creator-outreach.md`. Personalize the opening line for every single one. **Generic emails get 0% response. Personalized ones get 10-20%.**

Don't bulk-send. Send each one as you write it. 5 per evening over 3 evenings = 15 emails sent in week 3.

### 17. Drop Post 6 (Meet the bots) and Script 3 (Meet Lola Winnie) (Day 20-21)
This is your strongest v1.5 hook. The named-bots angle is uniquely Filipino and quotable. Post the carousel on Instagram, the Reel on TikTok.

## Week 4 — Press + sustain (4 hours over the week)

### 18. Send press pitches to 5-10 Filipino-American outlets (Day 22-23)
Use the **media outlet pitch** template from `04-creator-outreach.md`. Targets:
- Asian Journal (asianjournal.com — tech/lifestyle editor)
- The FilAm (thefilam.net)
- Bayanihan News
- Philippine News
- Filipino Reporter

Attach `press-kit.pdf` to every email. (Re-render it locally first with `render-press-kit.sh` so the fonts are right.)

Don't expect replies. Even one outlet picking it up is a win in week 4.

### 19. Drop remaining content from the pack across the week
- Day 22: Post 3 (Why I built this carousel) — your highest-converting story content
- Day 24: Post 7 (Climb the ranks) — showcases v1.5 progression
- Day 26: Post 8 (Daily challenges)
- Day 27: Script 4 (Personal story TikTok) — highest organic ceiling

### 20. Check your metrics (Day 28-30)
Open Play Console. Look at:
- Total installs since launch
- Average rating (you want 4.3+; if it's lower, find out why from reviews)
- Reviews count (aim for 30+ in month 1; 50+ is a strong launch)
- Country breakdown (gut-check that Filipino diaspora countries are showing up)

Don't optimize on impressions or follower count yet. **Installs + reviews are the only numbers that matter in month 1.**

## Beyond month 1

Once you're past day 30, the muscle to build is **rhythm, not bursts.** One Instagram post per week + one Reel per two weeks + one Reddit/forum engagement per week is sustainable and beats burnout cycles.

The other big thing to plan for: **iOS launch.** When iOS ships, treat it as a *second launch* — fresh announcements, fresh Reddit threads, fresh creator pitches with the new hook ("Now on iPhone!"). You get two launches for the cost of one.

These docs all have "iPhone coming soon" / "iOS in development" copy. When iOS goes live, run a find/replace:

| `index.html` | "Apple coming soon" → "Now on App Store" + add Apple badge link |

## Realistic expectations

Month 1 numbers for a well-executed solo indie launch in this niche:
- **50-300 installs** from organic + personal network
- **15-40 reviews** if you stay on top of asking
- **2-5 creator shares** if you send 15 warm pitches
- **0-1 press features**
- **A few hundred Instagram followers**

Anyone telling you to expect "viral overnight" is selling something. Steady growth in a defensible niche (Filipino mahjong is unique — there's no competition) compounds. Month 6 is when this gets interesting if you stay consistent.

## When in doubt

Reply to a comment. Reply to a review. Send a thank-you DM to someone who shared.

The marketing strategy is "be present." Everything else is execution.
