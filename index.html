THE FILIPINO RULES

Pinoy style. Welcome to all players — learn Filipino Mahjong in a round or two.

JOKERS

Wildcards that substitute for any suited tile (Balls, Char, or Sticks). Use them to Tódas, chase a Siete Pares, or build an Escalera.

FLOWERS

Be dealt zero flowers or collect all 13 for an instant hand-score bonus.

BÚNOT

Win by self-drawing your winning tile — doubles your hand score. Don't wait too long or Kuya will Tódas first.

SIETE PARES

Seven pairs plus one báhay. A high-skill win for ambitious players.

SAGÁSA

Extend your open Pung into a Kang when you draw the matching fourth tile. Adds to your hand score immediately.

BISAKLAT

Dealer dealt a complete winning hand at the start, before a single tile is played. The rarest and highest-value win in Filipino Mahjong.
